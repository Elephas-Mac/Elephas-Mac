## Overview

Elephas Professional Suite is a complete system-wide AI writing platform with knowledge base, templates, and automation for professional AI-assisted writing.

Super Brain knowledge base indexes your documents, notes, and uploaded files, enabling AI responses grounded in your personal materials. The knowledge base makes the AI aware of your projects, terminology, and context.

Writing templates define reusable AI instruction patterns for recurring writing tasks — meeting summaries, status updates, client responses — producing consistent outputs from standardized prompts.

Automation through Shortcuts integration enables AI writing as a step in larger automated workflows, processing text programmatically.

Elephas Professional Suite is the system-wide AI writing platform for professionals who need AI assistance embedded in their native workflow with the personalization and knowledge grounding that effective AI writing assistance requires.

## Performance & Integration

Elephas is engineered around macOS technologies that handle performance-critical work efficiently. Tasks run in the background without slowing down the rest of your system — you can keep working while Elephas processes operations quietly behind the scenes.

The app integrates deeply with the macOS ecosystem out of the box:

- Launch workflows directly through **Spotlight**
- Preview files instantly with **Quick Look**
- Trigger actions from any app via the **Services menu**
- Fully compatible with **Shortcuts** for automated workflows

## System Requirements

- macOS 12 Monterey or later
- Apple Silicon or Intel — Universal Binary
- Works with macOS Ventura, Sonoma and Sequoia
